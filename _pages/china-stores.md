<p class="block no-margin">Of course we don’t expect you to read and write Chinese so here’s a quick guide on how to search and read descriptions on the website.</p>
<p class="block">It would be best if you get on your desktop or laptop for this.</p>

<h3>Browsing on the site</h3>
<ol>
 	<li>Open a new tab or window and go to <a href="https://translate.google.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Google Translate</a>. Select “Detect Language” or “Chinese” on the drop down menu.</li>
 	<li>
<p class="block no-margin">Copy one of the Chinese URLs above and paste it into Google Translate text box on the left and hit the blue “Translate” button. It should produce a hyperlinked website on the left. Click that<img class="size-full wp-image-765 aligncenter" src="https://www.galleon.com.ph/wp-content/uploads/2018/11/1-china.png" alt="" width="971" height="191" /></p>
</li>
 	<li>You should see the website now translated to English.</li>
 	<li id="img-2" class="image">Repeat step 1 if you don’t see the Google Translate toolbar.<img class="aligncenter wp-image-766 size-large" src="https://www.galleon.com.ph/wp-content/uploads/2018/11/2-china-1024x632.png" alt="" width="960" height="593" /></li>
 	<li>If it shows an error or doesn’t load, you need to manually translate the text as you browse.</li>
</ol>
<h3>Translating Manually or via Smart Phone</h3>
<ol>
 	<li>Copy the Chinese words you would like to translate to English</li>
 	<li>
<p class="block no-margin">Open a new tab or window and go to <a href="https://translate.google.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Google Translate</a>. Select “Detect Language” or “Chinese” on the drop down menu. Then paste the Chinese words there and hit the blue “Translate” button<img class="size-large wp-image-768 aligncenter" src="https://www.galleon.com.ph/wp-content/uploads/2018/11/3-china-1024x250.png" alt="" width="960" height="234" /></p>
</li>
 	<li>
<p class="block no-margin">Another option is to download Google Translate on your <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/en/app/google-translate/id414706506?mt=8" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Apple Device (iOS)</a> or <a href="https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.google.android.apps.translate&amp;hl=en" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Android Device</a>. Select Chinese on the left drop-down menu. Then take a photo of the screen you are reading and highlight the words you want translated. This is great for text inside images<img class="aligncenter wp-image-771 size-full" src="https://www.galleon.com.ph/wp-content/uploads/2018/11/4-china.png" alt="" width="372" height="644" /></p>
</li>
</ol>
<h3>Searching for products</h3>
<ol>
 	<li>
<p class="block no-margin">Try searching in English keywords or brand names to get results. Note that for English words, you will only get results that have English words on the product name or description.<img class="size-full wp-image-772 aligncenter" src="https://www.galleon.com.ph/wp-content/uploads/2018/11/5-china.png" alt="" width="937" height="557" /></p>
</li>
 	<li id="img-6" class="image">Use <a href="https://translate.google.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Google Translate</a> but this time, switch that language so that it translates from English to Chinese.<img class="size-large wp-image-773 aligncenter" src="https://www.galleon.com.ph/wp-content/uploads/2018/11/6-china-1024x445.png" alt="" width="960" height="417" /></li>
 	<li>
<p class="block no-margin">Paste the Chinese words on the search bar of your preferred website and hit search. You can try a combination of Chinese and English words to see what comes up.<img class="size-large wp-image-774 aligncenter" src="https://www.galleon.com.ph/wp-content/uploads/2018/11/7-china-1024x498.png" alt="" width="960" height="467" /></p>
</li>
 	<li>
<p class="block no-margin">Most of these sites also have advanced search that can refine your search results to a particular brand, category, color, size, etc. Just use the Google Translate techniques above to be able to read the options.</p>
</li>
 	<li>
<p class="block no-margin">Some brand names in English is not translated properly. To find the Chinese brand equivalent, try searching for the English brand. Look at the listings that contains both English and Chinese translated brand on the product name. Copy the Chinese brand on the search bar.<img class="size-full wp-image-775 aligncenter" src="https://www.galleon.com.ph/wp-content/uploads/2018/11/8-china.png" alt="" width="957" height="576" /></p>

</li>
</ol>
